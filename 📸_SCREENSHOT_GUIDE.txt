💡 WHY THIS FIELD IS IMPORTANT:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Content variables (in HTML):
  {{verification_code}}    ← Shows code in email body
  {{to_name}}             ← Shows name in email body
  {{expiry_time}}         ← Shows expiration in email body

Recipient variable (in settings):
  {{to_email}}            ← Tells EmailJS WHERE to send!

The "To email" field is NOT in the HTML content.
It's in the SETTINGS at the TOP of the template page.
